# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

**Module:** 1
**Marks:** 20
**Role:** Student Digital Ambassador

---

## Overview

This task involved creating a one-page visual infographic to help batchmates understand what Digital Literacy means and why it matters in today's digital world.

---

## Tool Used

**Canva** (canva.com) — an online design platform used to create the infographic with a modular, visually engaging layout suitable for students.

---

## What the Infographic Covers

The infographic addresses the following five Digital Literacy topics:

1. **What is Digital Literacy?** — A definition and explanation of why digital literacy is essential for students today.
2. **Useful Digital Tools for Students** — Key tools including Google Workspace, Canva, and Notion that support academic and professional productivity.
3. **Safe Internet Practices** — Tips on creating strong passwords, adjusting privacy settings, recognising phishing attempts, and staying safe online.
4. **Professional Online Presence** — Guidance on building a LinkedIn profile, maintaining a positive digital footprint, and presenting yourself professionally online.
5. **Email Etiquette** — Best practices for writing clear, professional emails including appropriate subject lines, tone, and structure.

---

## Reflection

Creating this infographic using Canva was a straightforward process thanks to its drag-and-drop interface and pre-built design elements. One thing I found interesting was how challenging it was to balance visual clarity with information density — fitting five distinct topics onto a single page while keeping it readable and appealing required careful layout decisions. This exercise highlighted how design itself is a key component of digital literacy.
