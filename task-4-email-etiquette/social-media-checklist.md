# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ Do's

1. **Keep profiles professional** — Use a clear photo, consistent name, and
   an appropriate bio that reflects your academic and career goals.
2. **Think before you post** — Consider whether your content could affect
   your academic or professional reputation before sharing.
3. **Engage constructively** — Comment and share content that adds value,
   demonstrates knowledge, or supports others in your field.
4. **Use privacy settings** — Regularly review who can see your posts,
   personal details, and tagged content.
5. **Separate personal and professional accounts** — Keep a dedicated
   LinkedIn/GitHub for professional use and apply strict privacy to
   personal accounts.

## ❌ Don'ts

1. **Don't share sensitive information** — Never post your phone number,
   home address, college ID, or financial details publicly.
2. **Don't engage in online arguments** — Avoid heated public disputes;
   they can be screenshot and shared out of context.
3. **Don't plagiarise or repost without credit** — Always attribute
   original creators when sharing others' work or ideas.
4. **Don't overshare your location** — Avoid real-time location tagging
   or posting travel plans publicly.
5. **Don't ignore suspicious messages** — Never click unknown links or
   respond to strangers requesting personal information on social platforms.
