# Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist
## For College Students in India

### 🔒 General Online Safety
1. **Keep software and OS updated** — Always install security patches promptly.
   The WannaCry attack exploited an unpatched Windows vulnerability that had a
   fix available for months before the attack.
2. **Use strong, unique passwords** — Use a password manager (Bitwarden,
   KeePass) to generate and store complex passwords. Never reuse passwords
   across platforms.
3. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** — Always enable 2FA on email,
   social media, and banking apps using an authenticator app, not just SMS.
4. **Be cautious with public Wi-Fi** — Avoid accessing banking or sensitive
   accounts on public/college Wi-Fi. Use a VPN (ProtonVPN is free) if necessary.
5. **Back up your data regularly** — Maintain offline or cloud backups of
   important files. This is your best defence against ransomware.
6. **Verify links before clicking** — Hover over links to preview the URL.
   Avoid clicking shortened or suspicious links in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS.

### 💳 Financial / UPI Safety (India-Specific)
7. **Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or CVV** — No bank, UPI app, or government
   authority will ever ask for these. If someone does, it is a scam.
8. **Verify payment requests carefully** — Scammers send "collect" requests
   disguised as payments on UPI apps. Entering your PIN to "receive" money
   actually sends it. Always read the request type before entering your PIN.
9. **Use UPI apps only from official app stores** — Download PhonePe, GPay,
   or Paytm only from Google Play or App Store. Avoid APK links shared via
   WhatsApp or Telegram.
10. **Enable transaction alerts** — Keep SMS and in-app notifications active
    for all bank and UPI transactions so you can detect unauthorised activity
    immediately.

### 🚨 How to Report Cybercrime in India
- **National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
- **Cyber Crime Helpline:** 1930 (available 24/7)
- Report financial fraud within the first hour for best chance of fund recovery.
